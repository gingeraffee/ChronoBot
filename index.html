Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information Chromie collects and how it is used. By using the bot, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Receive

Through the Discord API, Chromie may receive and store:

  • Server (guild) IDs and names.
  • Channel IDs for the configured event channel.
  • Message IDs for pinned countdown messages.
  • Event details such as event names and event dates/times.
  • User IDs for users who link DMs to a specific server using bot commands.

Chromie does not intentionally store general message content beyond what is needed to process commands and operate countdown features.

How We Use This Information

We use the information we receive to:

  • Provide countdowns and reminder messages for your events.
  • Maintain a pinned countdown message in your chosen channel.
  • Allow configuration via slash commands and (optionally) direct messages.
  • Monitor stability, debug issues, and improve the bot's reliability.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Limited information may be shared with:

  • Hosting or infrastructure providers, solely to operate the bot.
  • Authorities or other parties if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Data Retention

Configuration and event data are kept as long as reasonably needed to operate Chromie for your server. If you remove the bot from your server, related data may be deleted as part of routine cleanup.
